Varenyky

Description

Varenyky (pronounced vah-rEH-nee-key) are Ukrainian stuffed dumplings. Potato-filled varenyky are the most traditional and theyâ€™re one of the simplest recipes I know. They call for just a handful of ingredients that everyone, no matter how limited in means, has access to: an egg, flour, potatoes, and onions.
